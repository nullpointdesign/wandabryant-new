Textile & fiber sculpture

3‑dimensional wall hangings and sculptures that explore color, texture, form, and movement.

Welcome. My 3-dimensional wall hangings and free-standing sculptures explore color, texture, form and movement through the manipulation of textiles in combination with found objects and natural elements such as tree branches, bark, and palm fronds and sheaths. Enjoy.

About

Read full statement ↗
I was born in Iowa; I now live with cats in Los Angeles. Following a long, successful academic career in ethnomusicology, I now work as a visual artist, specializing in sculpture, both freestanding and wall-mounted, with an emphasis on textile manipulation.
My works are layered with multiple meanings and often juxtapose LA street detritus with expensive fabrics to address the world I move through.
